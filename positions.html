Open Positions

We are currently building our team and welcome inquiries from outstanding candidates at all career stages.

The Doryab Lab is always interested in hearing from highly motivated undergraduate and graduate students, as well as postdoctoral researchers, who are passionate about translational organ microsystems, bioengineering, and human-relevant disease models.

If our research aligns with your interests, we encourage you to get in touch. Please send your CV, a brief statement of research interests, and any relevant supporting documents to Dr. Doryab at Email.

Prospective PhD students and postdoctoral researchers will receive strong support in identifying and applying for competitive external funding opportunities. Depending on eligibility and career stage, these may include programs such as the DFG Walter Benjamin Programme, the DAAD Fellowships, Alexander von Humboldt Foundation Fellowships, Marie Skłodowska-Curie, and other national and international funding schemes.

We welcome inquiries from candidates with backgrounds in biomedical engineering, bioengineering, biotechnology, life sciences, medicine, microfluidics, computational biology, and related disciplines.
